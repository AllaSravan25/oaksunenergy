FAQ

1. What is Solar EPC?

Solar EPC refers to the complete process of designing,procuring materials,and constructing solar power systems, including solar pannels, invertors, and other necessary components.

2. Why should I choose Solar EPC provider?

We OAKSUN ENERGY , Solar EPC provider offer comprehensive services, including system design, equipment sourcing, installation. and maintenance. Choosing OAKSUN an experienced EPC provider ensures a hassle-free and efficient solar installation process.

3. What services does a Solar EPC provider offer?

Solar EPC provider typically offer services such as site assessment,system design, Engineering, equipment Procurement, installation, testing, commissioning and ongoing system maintenance and monitoring.

4. How long does the Solar EPC process take?

The duration of the Solar EPC Process depends on the scale and complexity of the project. It typically involves several stages,including design,permitting,Procurement,installation, and commissioning. Generally it can take anywhere from 1 week to months.
